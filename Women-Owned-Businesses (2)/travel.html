Travel

Life's journeys start with what you can carry. These products are designed to be your perfect travel companions - functional, durable, and thoughtfully designed to make every trip easier. From luggage and bags to accessories and organizational tools, there is everything you need to pack smarter.

Away

Known for their modern and convenient travel accessories, the brand launched in 2015 from co-founders Jen Rubio and Stephanie Korey with the hardshell suitcases you’ve come to love.


Scout

Scout, founded by Deb Waterman Johns, is known for their bags being water-resistant, durable and lightweight, making them the perfect travel accessory



Flex-n-Fly

Known for aiding flight anxiety by providing stretching/yoga, and relaxation classes to travelers before they board their international and domestic flights.



CALPAK

Calpak offers sleek, affordable and highly functional travel accessories, a combination of luxury tote and a laptop bag.




BÉIS

BÉIS, founded by actress Shay Mitchell, offers products that are chic, multipurpose travel accessories that are affordable.


Lo & Sons

Hands-free carryall that is convertible and can be used as a belt bad, a wristlet, a crossbody or shoulder bag. Perfect for travel!


MZ WALLACE

Known for their on the go lightweight travel bags that are simultaneously stylish that range from nylon totes to crossbody bags.


STEAMLINE

Founded by Sara Banks in 2005, her goal was to create sophisticated pieces that signaled a more thoughtful, and more glamorous, approach to travel. Her unique collections come in sumptuous shades like burgundy and hunter green, and incorporate details like retro leather straps.
