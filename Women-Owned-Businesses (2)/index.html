Support Women-Owned Businesses

Over the decades, women-owned businesses has significantly increased and to this date, 42% of U.S. businesses are owned by women. These entrepenuers have left their 9 to 5 jobs, striving towawrds their passion to create products that serve the greater good. Yet, many still face challenges that impact the growth of their company, including access to funding and capital, discrimination and bias, and gendered assumptions.
